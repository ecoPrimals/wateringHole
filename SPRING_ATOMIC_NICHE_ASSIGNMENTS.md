# Spring Atomic Niche Assignments

**Date**: May 13, 2026
**Version**: 1.0
**Status**: Active
**Phase**: Phase 32 atomic model — interstadial convergence

---

## Concept

Each delta spring occupies a **niche** within the NUCLEUS atomic model,
stress-testing a specific primal composition before it flows downstream to
sporeGarden products. This is functional niche partitioning — springs
specialize on the atomic tier that aligns with their domain, ensuring every
composition path is validated by at least two springs before downstream
handoff.

The ecology analogy is precise: hotSpring is a literal hydrothermal vent
community — operating on its own energy source (GPU hardware) while the
broader ecosystem runs on the standard IPC pipeline. healthSpring occupies
the provenance niche — clinical data integrity demands the full Nest atomic.
ludoSpring stress-tests Tower because interactive products exercise trust,
crypto, and defense harder than any science spring.

## Assignments

### Atomic Specialists (primary stress-test owners)

| Atomic | Particle | Niche Spring | Domain Rationale |
|--------|----------|-------------|------------------|
| **Tower** (electron) | Trust, crypto, discovery, defense | **ludoSpring** | Games need all Tower capabilities — BTSP auth for multiplayer sessions, Songbird mesh for peer discovery, skunkBat defense for anti-cheat. Interactive products stress Tower harder than any science spring. |
| **Node** (proton) | Compute, tensor, shader dispatch | **hotSpring** | Thermal niche — GPU hardware, compute trio sovereignty, VFIO/PBDMA. Own cadence, own hardware. hotSpring on biomeGate owns sovereign compute execution. |
| **Nest** (neutron) | Storage, DAG, ledger, attribution | **healthSpring** | Clinical data provenance — NestGate content pipeline, rhizoCrypt DAG for audit trails, loamSpine ledger for patient record integrity, sweetGrass for attribution chain. Ionic bridge to dual-tower enclave is Nest's most complex deployment. |

### Cross-Atomic Validators (secondary stress-test, composition parity)

| Composition | Niche Spring | Domain Rationale |
|-------------|-------------|------------------|
| **Tower + Node** | **airSpring** | Atmospheric modeling: crypto-authenticated sensor feeds (Tower) dispatched to GPU compute (Node). Real-time processing doesn't need full Nest provenance. |
| **Tower + Nest** | **groundSpring** | LTEE reproductions: crypto-verified data provenance (Tower auth + Nest storage). Compute is CPU-bound fitness calculations. Data integrity is the core value proposition. |

### Full Composition Validators

| Composition | Niche Spring | Domain Rationale |
|-------------|-------------|------------------|
| **Full NUCLEUS** (atom) | **wetSpring** | Highest test count (1,613+), broadest domain (environment = everything interacts). Needs all three atomics for EPA/NCBI data pipelines. Natural stress-test for full composition. guideStone L5 (May 13). |
| **Meta-tier** (biomeOS + Squirrel + petalTongue) | **neuralSpring** | AI inference is the meta-tier's primary consumer — Squirrel routing, biomeOS orchestration, petalTongue visualization of model outputs. |

### Coordinator

| Role | Spring | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| **Coordination** | **primalSpring** | Validates all compositions, surfaces gaps, provides validation library. Does not own a specific atomic niche — owns the composition validation of all niches. |

## Downstream Handoff

When a spring validates its atomic niche, the composition pattern is
considered stress-tested and ready for downstream handoff to sporeGarden
products. The handoff path:

```
Spring (niche validation) → primalSpring (composition validation) → wateringHole (pattern docs) → sporeGarden (product deployment)
```

Products (foundation, lithoSpore, projectNUCLEUS) consume validated
compositions — they don't need to re-validate the atomic tier, only their
domain-specific usage of it.
