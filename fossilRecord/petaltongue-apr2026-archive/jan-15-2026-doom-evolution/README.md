## 🎓 **Lessons Learned**

1. **Test-Driven Evolution Works**
   - Use real integration as probe

   - Solve systematically

3. **Architecture Emerges**
   - Don't speculate
   - Build, discover, evolve
   - Document learnings

   - Result > sum of parts
