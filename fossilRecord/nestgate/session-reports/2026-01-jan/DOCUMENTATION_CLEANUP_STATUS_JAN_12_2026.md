## 💡 LESSONS DOCUMENTED

### Estimation Lessons

1. **Module Size Matters**: Can't extrapolate from small modules to whole codebase
2. **Measure Frequently**: Reality check needed after each batch
3. **Conservative Estimates**: Better to under-promise and over-deliver
4. **Weighted Impact**: Consider module size in coverage calculations

### Communication Lessons

1. **Honesty First**: Better to acknowledge mistakes openly
2. **Explain Why**: Help understand reasoning, not just results
3. **Stay Positive**: Real progress is still progress
4. **Set Realistic Goals**: Credibility > optimism
