```rust
// 1. SELF-AWARENESS (Birth - know only myself)

    .with_name("nestgate")
    .with_capability("storage")
    .build()?;

// 2. AUTO-DETECT DISCOVERY (Learning to see)

    .find_by_capability("orchestration")
```
